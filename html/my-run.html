My Races - So Far!

Nordic Race - Beach 2023!

My very first OCR race - Nordic Race Beach 2023

This race marked the beginning of my OCR journey. It was a challenging experience, where I truly had to fight my way through the obstacles. Right from the start, I encountered hills and obstacles that required both strength and endurance. I was close to giving up several times, especially as fatigue and the challenges began to wear down my motivation.

I took three penalty rounds for the obstacles I couldn't overcome, and although it was frustrating, it also gave me a sense of resilience. Crossing the finish line was a triumph; it felt like climbing a mountain I didnâ€™t think I could overcome. One of the most memorable moments was when I jumped into the water. It was refreshing and a welcome break from the intense struggle.

Even after the race, when I was tired and exhausted, I couldn't help but smile when I thought of the sense of freedom I got from diving into the water. It felt like letting go of all stress and fatigue. This race taught me a lot about my limits and my will to overcome them, and I look forward to many more challenges in the future.

Nordic Race - Beach 2024!

Nordic Race Beach 2024

A year later, I participated in the same race, and this time it went much better. With more experience and more energy, I was able to tackle the obstacles with confidence and determination. It was a big difference from my first race, where I struggled to get through.

Overcoming the obstacles was not only a physical achievement, but also a mental triumph. Every time I jumped over a wall or climbed up a slope, I felt a wave of pride and joy. It was amazing to see how much I had developed since my first race, and it motivated me to push myself even harder. I realized that with hard work and dedication, I could achieve my goals and more.

Reborn - Family Run 2024!

Reborn Family Run 2024

In the same month, I participated in my first family race, Reborn Family Run, with my 10-year-old daughter. It was a truly special experience to share this challenge with her. Being together in that way created memories we both will cherish forever.

Although it was a long race with quite a bit of waiting, it was worth it to see her face the challenges with courage and joy. Every time we faced an obstacle, I could feel her excitement and determination, making it an incredible experience for me as a father. It was not just a race; it was a journey we took together, filled with laughter, teamwork, and a stronger bond between us.

Nordic Race - Urban 2024!

Nordic Race Urban 2024

My latest race was Nordic Race Urban, where I had originally planned to run twice. I was asked if I would help some first-time runners, which I found very inspiring. It was a chance for me to give back to the OCR community.

Unfortunately, I got a concussion two weeks before, so it turned into a more relaxed race where I focused on helping others. Although it wasn't the challenge I had been looking forward to, it became a valuable experience. I enjoyed seeing the new participants overcome their obstacles and sharing their excitement about being part of the race.

You can read more about it here: Nordic Race Urban. Now I'm looking forward to 2025, when I can hopefully go all out again and take on the challenges OCR has to offer.
